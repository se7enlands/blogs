Galápagos – Red Beach

加拉帕戈斯群岛 – 红沙滩

-------------



拉维达岛最与众不同的景观就是它的红色沙滩，沙质细腻，沙面平坦。白色的海浪拍打着红色的沙滩，这样的美景在世界各地的沙滩中也能排的上号。



The most unique feature of Rábida island is its fine red sand beach. The white waves lap up against the red sand, a beauty that would be ranked on the top among all the beaches of the world.

<p>拉维达岛（Rábida）位于圣地亚哥岛的南侧，距离只有4.5公里。岛的面积很小，火山活动形态丰富。拉维达岛的一圈几乎都是火山崖，只有北面的沙滩适合于访客登陆。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>拉维达岛上几座火山的岩浆中富含铁元素，这赋予了小岛独特的红棕色调。接近登陆点，红色的悬崖上有蓝脚鲣鸟（Blue-footed booby）在筑巢。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->

[singlepic id=3661 w=840 h=640 float=center]悬崖上歇脚的岩鸥和仙人掌[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>拉维达岛最与众不同的景观就是它的红色沙滩，沙质细腻，沙面平坦。白色的海浪拍打着红色的沙滩，这样的美景在世界各地的沙滩中也能排的上号。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3653 w=840 h=640 float=center]拉维达岛的红色沙滩[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>我们登上沙滩右侧的小山，天有些放晴。从这里可以清楚的看到沙滩后面的泻湖。山坡上也是长满Palo Santo树。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3651 w=840 h=640 float=center]紧挨沙滩的泻湖[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>沿着山上的小路向前走，天空时晴时阴，有一阵还下起小雨。海岸边的红色火山岩上顽强的生长着一片片的仙人掌（Prickly Pear Cactus），Marco说它们像是米老鼠的耳朵。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3664 w=840 h=640 float=center]拉维达岛的小路[/singlepic]
[singlepic id=3652 w=840 h=640 float=center]红色火山岩上的仙人掌[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>拉维达岛上能走的小路并不长，不一会儿，我们就转回了红沙滩。沙滩绕过山坡继续延伸，沙滩上不但可以看到海狮、海鬣蜥这些加拉帕戈斯群岛上常见的动物，鹈鹕（pelican）在岸边筑巢，这里是近距离观察它们的好地方。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3660 w=840 h=640 float=center]岸边筑巢的鹈鹕[/singlepic]
[singlepic id=3655 w=840 h=640 float=center]拉维达岛沙滩上的海狮[/singlepic]
[singlepic id=3654 w=840 h=640 float=center]红色岩石上孤独的海鬣蜥[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>与众不同的红沙滩总能给平淡无奇的风景平添一种韵味。沙滩的尽头是一道高耸的悬崖，仿佛世界到此为止，给人天涯海角的感觉。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3657 w=840 h=640 float=center]沙滩上样貌独特的石头[/singlepic]
[singlepic id=3663 w=840 h=640 float=center]沙滩尽头的悬崖[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>最后不得不赞的是为我们打扫房间的船员。每次登陆回来，不但床铺被收拾的整整齐齐，船员还用毛巾叠成各式的动物形状，可见船员工作的用心和热情。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3650 w=840 h=640 float=center]房间床铺上用毛巾叠成的大象[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>午饭前，我们离开拉维达岛，向东北方向驶去。我们的下一站是中国帽子岛（Sombrero Chino）。 </p>

<p>Rábida island is located less 3 miles to the south of Santiago island. The island is small in size and rich in volcanic activity patterns. Almost the entire perimeter of the island are volcanic cliffs, and only the beach on the north is suitable for visitors to land.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The lava from several volcanoes on Rábida island is rich in iron, which gives the island its distinctive reddish-brown hue. Near the landing site, the red cliffs are home to nesting Blue-footed boobies.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The most unique feature of Rábida island is its fine red sand beach. The white waves lap up against the red sand, a beauty that would be ranked on the top among all the beaches of the world.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3653 w=840 h=640 float=center]Red beach of Rábida Island[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The sky was clearing somewhat as we climbed the hill to the right of the beach. From here you can clearly see the lagoon behind the beach. The hill is also full of Palo Santo trees.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3651 w=840 h=640 float=center]The lagoon right behind the beach, Rábida Island[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As we walked along the path up the hill, it started raining for a while. The red volcanic rocks along the coast were covered with patches of Prickly Pear Cactus, which Marco said they looked like the ears of Mickey Mouse.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3664 w=840 h=640 float=center]The hiking trail of Rábida Island[/singlepic]
[singlepic id=3652 w=840 h=640 float=center]Cactus on the volcanic rocks[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The walkable trails on Rábida Island are not very long. We turned back to the beach in 30 minutes. The beach continues around the hillside. There are sea lions and marine iguanas lying on the beach. Pelicans are nesting on the shore and this is a great place to see them up close.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[singlepic id=3660 w=840 h=640 float=center]A pelican nesting on the rock, Rábida Island[/singlepic]

[singlepic id=3654 w=840 h=640 float=center]A lonely marine iguanas on the volcanic rocks, Rábida Island[/singlepic]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The distinctive red sand beach always adds a charm to the  landscape. At the end of the beach is a towering cliff, as if the world ends here.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The last thing I have to say is the crew who cleaned our room. Every time we came back from landing, not only were the beds neatly cleaned, but the crew also folded towels into various animal shapes, which shows the crew's dedication and enthusiasm for their work.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
